üìä What is the Technology Health Score?

The Health Score (0‚Äì100) rates a website's technology stack based on:

  • Modernity ‚Äî Are frameworks up to date?

  • Security ‚Äî HTTPS, secure practices

üíæ Where is my scan history stored?

All scan history is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage. Nothing is sent to our servers. You can clear your history at any time from within the extension.

üì§ How do I export my scan history?

Open the extension, go to the History tab, and click the Export CSV button. The file will download to your device.

üîí Does the extension collect any of my data?

No. Tech Stack Detector does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All processing happens entirely in your browser. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

üåê Why does it need access to all websites?

The extension uses a content script to read the page's HTML, scripts, and stylesheets to identify technologies. This requires access to the pages you visit. The extension only runs when you click its icon ‚Äî it does not monitor your browsing in the background.

üõ†Ô∏è A technology isn't being detected correctly

Our detection database covers 150+ technologies, but some sites use custom setups that are harder to fingerprint. If you've found a missed or incorrect detection, please open an issue on GitHub with the URL and the expected technology ‚Äî we'll add it to the next update.

üóëÔ∏è How do I uninstall the extension?
  1. Right-click the extension icon in Chrome's toolbar
  2. Select Remove from Chrome
  3. Confirm removal

This will remove all locally stored scan history as well.

Troubleshooting

Extension not appearing in toolbar

Click the puzzle piece icon (üß©) in Chrome's toolbar to see all extensions. Click the pin icon next to Tech Stack Detector to pin it to your toolbar.

Extension showing outdated data

Click the Rescan button in the popup to force a fresh scan of the current page.

Extension stopped working after Chrome update

Try disabling and re-enabling the extension in chrome://extensions, or uninstalling and reinstalling it from the Chrome Web Store.
